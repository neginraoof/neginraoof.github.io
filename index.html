Negin Raoof

I’m (super excited to be!) a first-year ECE PhD student at UT Austin, advised by Alex Dimakis. My research focuses on multi-task learning in generative language models and its implications for robustness to adversarial perturbations. My work is inspired by cognitive science studies on bilingualism.

Prior to joining the PhD program, I was a software engineer || at Microsoft working on: Siphon: Streaming data ingestion with Apache Kafka, and PyTorch - ONNX .
